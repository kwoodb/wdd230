Site Name and Domain

The site name will be High Country Weather and Events with the domain of www.hcweatherandevents.com.

Site Purpose

The main purposes of the High Country Weather and Events website will be to facilitate parents and teachers in educating elementary aged children regarding local weather and to communicate regional information to individuals living in the connected communities. This website will be used in public schools in the Southeastern region of Idaho, specifically in Fish Haven, Soda Springs, and Preston, Idaho. Teachers will utilize the website during the curriculum's weather units and students will be able to access the website (with parental supervision) to complete at home assignments. Additionally, community members can stay informed on local announcemnts and events.

Logo

The website is an educational tool for elementary age children learning about weather and for community members to stay informed and connected. The sun logo was chosen because of its simplicity and because it suggests positivity.
