Turkey Trot

by Koralee Waite

Looking for something fun to do while the Turkey is roasting? Bring your turkey legs and race for a cause. This year the annual Turkey Trot is donating it's proceeds to the local shelter to provide Christmas gifts for underpriviledged families in our community. Spread Christmas cheer while burning pre-Thanksgiving calories. Sign-ups are taking place at the elementary school Monday - Friday until race day, 25 November 2021!
