The West Richland Chamber of Commerce is here to serve you and your business needs. We are here to provide up-to-date local and area information that could impact your business, facilitate networking opportunities, and vocalize the issues your business is facing.

Northwest Rentals

Christmas is just around the corner. Are you still looking for a gift? How about a new 4 wheeler? Northwest Rentals is holding their annual 4 wheeler giveaway! Entry is as easy as reserving your 2022 rentals. The more rentals you reserve, the more entries you gain!

Yokes Fresh Market: Giving Tree

Looking for a way to give back this season? Look no further than your local Yokes Fresh Market. Their yearly Giving Tree will begin November 26 and run through December 20. Serve your community by helping underpriviledged children experience a magical Christmas.

Tri-Cities Engineers: Christmas Village

Every year Tri-Cities Engineers holds their annual event, Christmas Village, in which they transform their offices into a winter wonderland. Join them on December 18th from 9-5 for an exprience you will never forget! Santa and three reindeer will make appearances througout the day.
